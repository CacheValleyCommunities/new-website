How to Spot and Avoid Scams
Scams use urgency, emotional manipulation, and too-good-to-be-true offers to deceive victims. Avoid falling for them by verifying sources, being cautious with unsolicited messages, and protecting personal information. Watch for red flags like suspicious payment methods and unprofessional communication. Stay vigilant, trust your instincts, and report any suspicious activity.
