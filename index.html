Why does this site look like this?

Simple, most of our users are technical people. And, this matches what they're use to seeing in code editors, browsers, applications, and more.


Additionally, we wanted to make sure our site loads for everyone. Meaning we can't have the latest and greatest website technology and styling. We depend on basic HTML, CSS, and JS. The result? Even computers from 2010 can render our page. (though parts might be a bit visually broken)
