Cache Tech Community - STEAM Education Roundtable - February 2026

February 12, 2026 Communities

Facilitated discussion on STEAM education, community involvement, and shared opportunities for students

Event Details:

  • Date: Saturday, February 28th, 2026
  • Time: 1:30 PM – 3:30 PM (MST)
  • Location: Bridgerland Technical College, West Building, Room 1860, 1410 N 1000 W, Logan, UT 84321

Event Overview

Join us for a facilitated discussion focused on STEAM education, community involvement, and shared opportunities for students. This roundtable aims to bring together educators (public and charter), students, and members of the local tech and professional community to have an open, commitment-free conversation about:

  • Challenges and needs in STEAM education
  • Ways community members can support educators and students
  • The mutual benefits of engagement between education and industry

The format is a whole-group, facilitator-led discussion. This is not a presentation or recruitment event, and no commitments are expected. The goal is to listen, learn from one another, and explore how stronger connections between education and the local community can benefit students of all backgrounds.

What You’ll Learn

In this roundtable discussion, you’ll have the opportunity to:

  • Share your perspective on STEAM education challenges and opportunities
  • Learn how community members can support educators and students
  • Explore ways to strengthen connections between education and industry

Why Attend This Meetup?

Show up if you’re:

  • Educators looking to share challenges and collaborate with the community
  • Students interested in STEAM opportunities and community support
  • Tech professionals wanting to engage with education and share insights
  • Community members eager to support local education initiatives
  • Anyone passionate about STEAM education and its impact on the community

Connect With Us

Join our Discord community to:

  • Get presentation updates and materials
  • Connect with other meetup attendees
  • Share tips and techniques
  • Stay updated on future community meetups and events
  • Continue learning and discussions after the meetup
